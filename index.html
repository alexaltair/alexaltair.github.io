About

I am a truth-seeker, an agent foundations researcher, and a member of humanity. I believe that we have the potential for unbounded flourishing, and that we can maximize our chances by embracing an intricate understanding of reality and striving for ever-broader coordination.

This is a collection of my writing, my attempts to communicate ideas that I think will help lead us to a better future, or sometimes just parts of the world that I find fascinating.
